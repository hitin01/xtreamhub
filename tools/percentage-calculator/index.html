Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Percentage calculations.

A percentage is a number expressed as a fraction of 100. It is commonly used to compare values, calculate discounts, marks, interest, and many other mathematical problems.

Percentage is calculated by dividing the part by the whole and then multiplying the result by 100.

Percentage increase measures how much a value has grown compared to its original value.

Percentage decrease shows how much a value has reduced compared to its original value.
