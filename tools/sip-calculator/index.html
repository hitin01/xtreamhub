Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about EMI calculations.

SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) is a simple way to invest a fixed amount of money regularly in mutual funds. It helps you build wealth over time through disciplined investing and the power of compounding.

The SIP Calculator estimates the future value of your investment based on your monthly investment amount, expected annual return, and investment period. It uses the standard compound interest formula to calculate your estimated maturity value.

SIP and Fixed Deposits (FDs) serve different financial goals. SIPs have the potential to generate higher long-term returns but are subject to market risks. FDs offer fixed and guaranteed returns with lower risk. Choose the option that best matches your financial goals and risk tolerance.
